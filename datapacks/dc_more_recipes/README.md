# More Recipes

**More Recipes** adds new recipes to the game, which give items new uses or provide new ways to obtain them.

## Anvil Repair

Anvils can be **repaired** by one stage by surrounding them with Iron Ingots.

## Moss Variants

Mossy versions of **cobblestone** and **stone brick** stairs, slabs, and walls can be crafted by combining non-mossy counterparts with a Vine or Moss Block, just like full blocks.

## Uncompressing Ice

Blue Ice can be uncompressed into 9 Packed Ice, which can each be compressed into 9 Ice (meaning that Blue Ice can be converted into 81 Ice).

## Rotten Flesh Recycling

Rotten flesh can be **dried** in a Furnace or Campfire to recycle it into **Rabbit Hide**, which provides an alternative means for crafting **Leather**! This takes much longer than most items to smelt.

## Charcoal Dye

**Charcoal** can be crafted into **Black Dye**.
